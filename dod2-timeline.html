DOD MANIAX The Eighteen Years Leading Towards DOD2

Time Period Events People
DOD The breaking of the Seals results in the collapse of the dimensional barriers. The dimensional equilibrium is lost along with its barrier.

Any creature that enters the Seeds is reborn as a monster created for the purpose of eradicating humanity, but the Gods plans go off course when Inuart, and the Goddess, Furiae, enters the Seed. The being that had been created as a result of this is Nowe, an incredibly double-edged being who carries the potential to either destroy humanity, or choose another alternative.

Angelus becomes the Goddess, and the world is sealed. 		Furiae becomes the Goddess

Caim and Angelus form a pact

Inuart and the Black Dragon (Legna) form a pact

Furiae passes away

Inuart passes away

Angelus becomes the Goddess

Following the end of DOD The Cult of the Watchers (The Imperial Army) collapses. The Imperial soldiers also recover from the Red Eye Disease.

The Union, having lost its purpose of overthrowing the Empire, begins to follow its own interests, fighting to take advantage of the disorder and mayhem in various nations. 		Eris and Nowe are born.

Gismor (26 Years old)

Oror (34 Years old)

Hanch (14 Years old) already in a pact

Verdelet takes guardianship over Seere in the Silent Forest.

Caim brings Manah with him, wandering various places.

Two years later The mayhem in spread across the different countries begins to subside.

Verdelet forms the Knights of the Seal. It’s original purpose is to guard the Seals, protect the priests, and provide research into the Seals.

Five years later Research is started into the undulation of the Great Time.

The Promised Land is discovered. The Promised Land is a dimensional singularity, an umbilical cord, an access point for divine power. There are no Seeds in the Promised Land.

The Castle of the Goddess is designated as the Holy Land, and deemed an inviolable area.

Six years later The Sealing Plan is put into operation. Because they could no longer manage the Goddess like they used to, a plan was set in place to prevent the dragon from breaking the seal by creating a man-made, artificial seal to lock it from the outside and therefore strengthen it’s power.

An attempt is made to utilise the waves of the Great Time. The placement of Crystals in the Promised Land begins, and start to be placed surrounding the Promised Land. Crystals are converters for the waves of the Great Time. Airships and other motive powers use the power that flows from these crystals.

The plan to spiritually restrain Angelus and steal her freedom is put into motion, separating and binding her soul. The constrained senses and locations are not synchronised together, and so each sense is freed in the following order regardless of which location it was released from: Hearing, speech, sight, figure, and movement.

In order to establish districts placed under their direct control, the Knights of the Seal begin to govern neighbouring countries.

The immense agony Angelus felt from the Sealing Plan’s separation of her soul reaches Caim, after which all contact between the two is lost.

Manah (12 years old) escapes from Caim. While on the run, she loses her memory, and is taken in by an ex-Imperial village (Manah is not recognised as Priestess since the villagers had recovered from the Red Eye Disease. No one had seen her to begin with.). She learned the hard truth as she saw the oppression of the people within the village. There, she is brought up by a mage who teaches her necessary life skills and magic. Afterwards, they act together until she is about 20 years old.

Caim embarks on an aimless journey in search of Angelus.

Seere (12 Years old) becomes a priest.

Gismor (37 Years old), who was in charge of the District of Heavenly Time, forms a pact. He becomes the first Guardian of the Key (Verdelet’s dogmatic precedence had left Oror unaware of how the Seal system relied on those who had entered into a pact). Due to this achievement, he became General Gismor.

Seven years later Following the success of Gismor’s experiment, Verdelet and Gismor search for more pact-bearers for the Sealing Plan.

Urick joins the Knights (16 Years old)

Eris is taken in by the Knights (7 Years old)

Gismor finds Hanch, and enlists her in the Knights (21 Years old)

Eight years later Seere informs an oracle that a saviour will appear in the Forgotten Ruins. Nowe is discovered there. In ancient times, the Forgotten Ruins were where the tribe of Holy Dragons descended to Earth. Currently, it was little more than marshlands.

Nowe is found (8 Years old), and taken in by the Knights.

Legna and Nowe go to join the Knights together.

Nine years later Yaha joins the Knights (16 Years old)

Eleven years later The separation of Angelus’ soul is completed. Districts placed under direct control of the Knights are created as a kind of vein for the spiritual energy that is stored within each one. The Forgotten Ruins are located within the District of Hallowed Water, and the ruins in other districts, such as the District of Soul Flame are connected with this.

Fourteen Years Later Nowe’s first battle (14 Years old). Becomes an apprentice to the Knights.

Zhangpo joins the Knights (16 Years old), and becomes an apprentice. In the same year, he forms a pact.

Eris becomes a woman (14 Years old).
Fifteen Years Later Caim attacks the District of Shining Life. Urick, who happened to be present during the attack, was a troop member for the District of Shining Life. His rank was as a Junior Knight. The District system is completed. Gismor establishes political power among the Knights. Urick makes a pact with the Reaper. Oror dies trying to protect the District (48 Years old).

Verdelet passes away.
Fifteen Years Later Nowe becomes a full-fledged knight.
