Drag-On Dragoon 2 Web Material

Web Material was a CD given out during prerelease promotional events for Drakengard 2 in a first-come first serve basis in limited quantities. It was also used for promotional material by representitives. That being said, I was very lucky to be able to obtain it awhile back, one of my favourite pieces in my collection.





Anyways, its content is slightly underwhelming compared to what you can find in the artbooks, but it is interesting. It contains 3 trailers, pages for the characters (excluding Caim and Angelus for some reason), and information about the gameplay.
